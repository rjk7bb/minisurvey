Quickly, on paper, draw your group as a network. Each person (including yourself) should be one circle, and add lines of varying thickness to show connections between people.
For instance, two people who worked closely should have a thick line between them while two people who did not work that closely should have a thin line. Be sure to label who you were in the network.
Also label your name and role in the top right corner of the paper.
